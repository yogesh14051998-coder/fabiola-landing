Our Mission

Hope, Healing & Justice

We walk with survivors of sexual assault and human trafficking through every step — meeting people where they are, with compassion and care.

Support

Compassionate, survivor-centered support — a safe space, a listening ear, and connection to the resources people need.

Empower

Mentoring, coaching, and advocacy that help survivors reclaim their voice, their confidence, and their future.

Heal

A path toward healing and justice — restoring dignity, peace, and the freedom to build a life of opportunity.

About Fabiola

Entrepreneur, mentor & humanitarian

Fabiola Acevedo is an entrepreneur, luxury real estate professional since 2009, with a background as a Financial Analyst and in lending — and, before that, cleaning houses and modeling in Chile. Today she is a mentor, coach, and humanitarian dedicated to helping individuals, organizations, and communities reach their highest potential.

Committed to lifelong learning, she has invested in coaching and leadership development with some of the world's most respected mentors. She believes true leadership begins with humility, integrity, and a willingness to keep growing — and that compassion can overcome division.
