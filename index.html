Meet Fabiola

A Trusted Advisor in Luxury

Fabiola Acevedo is a luxury real estate entrepreneur, investor, and community leader with more than 25 years of experience in the real estate industry. As a proud member of Keller Williams Luxury, she brings unmatched market expertise and a global network to every client. Originally from Chile and based in South Florida, she has built a successful career advising clients in the luxury residential market while leading purpose-driven initiatives focused on leadership, empowerment, and personal transformation.


Throughout her career, Fabiola has been recognized for her commitment to integrity, diversity, and conscious leadership. Her work blends business excellence with a strong focus on human development — helping individuals and communities thrive through vision, resilience, and purpose.
